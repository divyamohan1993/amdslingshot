# AMD Ryzen AI Slingshot - Campus Days Structure

## Overview

Slingshot Campus Days are two-day offline events held across **10 Indian cities**. Each city event consists of two distinct components:

---

## Day 1: Regional Ideathon

**Format:** One-day, offline-only innovation sprint

**Key Details:**
- **Standalone participation** — teams apply separately from the main hackathon track, compete only on event day
- **Self-contained** — no prior or post-event commitments; everything happens in a single day
- Teams brainstorm, build, and pitch solutions during the event
- **Winners announced on the spot** with a dedicated prize pool
- Only shortlisted teams from city-wise Ideathon applications are invited
- Separate applications from the main hackathon track
- Dedicated prize pool exclusive to this track

---

## Day 2: Regional Demo Day

**Format:** Prototype showcase to expert jury

**Key Details:**
- **"Exclusive for Slingshot Teams — Only shortlisted teams from the AMD Slingshot contest are eligible"**
- Teams who submitted ideas online and cleared the shortlisting process present their prototypes
- Solutions evaluated by **industry leaders and AMD professionals**
- Evaluation based on **innovation, feasibility, and impact**
- **"From each city, one team will be shortlisted to advance to the National Grand Finale"**
- Expert guidance and mentorship provided during the event

## Path to Grand Finale

1. Online prototype submission → Shortlisting (16 March 2026)
2. RSVP and Briefing (16–26 March 2026)
3. Regional Demo Day at assigned city → One team per city advances
4. Virtual Mentorship & Pitch Refinement (6 May 2026)
5. National Grand Finale (23 May 2026, offline at national venue)
