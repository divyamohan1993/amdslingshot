# AMD Ryzen AI Slingshot - Frequently Asked Questions

## Q1: Who can participate in the AMD Slingshot?

"Only students currently enrolled in universities, colleges, or institutes across India are eligible."

---

## Q2: Do I need to have a team to register?

"One needs to register solo and then form a team of up to 3 members. You can also participate solo."

---

## Q3: Is there a registration fee?

"No, participation in AMD Ryzen AI Slingshot is completely free."

---

## Q4: Will mentorship be provided?

"Yes. Shortlisted teams will receive mentorship and guidance from AMD experts and industry professionals."

---

## Q5: Can students outside India participate?

"No. The Contest is exclusively for students studying at campuses in India."

---

## Q6: How will the shortlisted teams be informed?

"Teams will be notified via email and official communication channels."

---

## Q7: Where will the Grand Finale take place?

"The Grand Finale will be held offline at a national venue. Finalists will receive complete details in advance."
