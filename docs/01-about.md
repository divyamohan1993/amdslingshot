# AMD Ryzen AI Slingshot - About

## What is AMD Slingshot?

**Official Name:** AMD Ryzen AI Slingshot

**Tagline:** "Slingshot is a national startup idea challenge, encouraging students to develop AI-powered solutions for real-world challenges."

AMD provides mentorship and workshops to help participants bring their concepts to life, aiming to cultivate the upcoming generation of AI innovators.

**Organizer:** Advanced Micro Devices, Inc. (AMD)
**Platform Partner:** Hack2Skill (H2S)

**Format:** A premier nationwide student challenge and Startup Idea Challenge aimed at empowering college innovators to design AI-driven solutions for real-world problems, spanning three months and 10 cities.

**Cost:** Completely free. No registration fee.

## Four-Stage Journey

1. **Registration** — "Assemble a team of your own with like-minded innovators"
2. **Idea Submission** — "Pick a theme and craft a unique solution"
3. **Slingshot Campus Day** — "With experts helping them reach their ideas to its highest potential"
4. **Grand Finale** — "Best performing teams will compete nationally"
