## Key Patterns in Winning Projects

1. **Working Prototype > Slides** — Functional demo beats theoretical ideas
2. **Clear Problem-Solution Fit** — Real, specific problem with AI solving it better than alternatives
3. **Innovation** — Novel approach, not just wrapping an API
4. **Feasibility** — Demonstrates technical viability and realistic deployment path
5. **Real-World Impact** — Quantifiable outcomes showing who benefits and how many
6. **AMD Technology Leverage** — Using ROCm, Ryzen AI, Developer Cloud, MI300X
7. **Strong Pitch & Demo** — Clear, concise, compelling presentation
8. **Ethics & Responsibility** — Built-in bias checks, privacy-by-default, accessibility
9. **Scalability Story** — Campus prototype that could scale to community, city, or national level
10. **Team Composition** — Complementary skills in coding, design, and business
