# AMD Ryzen AI Slingshot - Eligibility & Team Requirements

## Eligibility Criteria

- Open **exclusively** to undergraduate and postgraduate students enrolled in Indian colleges and universities
- All participants must be at least **18 years old**
- Students outside India **cannot** participate — the contest is exclusively for those studying at Indian campuses
- International students are ineligible
- Currently enrolled status required

## Team Requirements

- Teams of **up to 3 students** from any discipline (technical, design, or business) are welcome
- **Solo participation** is also permitted
- Registration begins individually, then participants form groups of up to 3 members
- Teams can include students from any field — technical, design, business, etc.
- No cross-college restriction mentioned — the site does not state that all team members must be from the same institution
- Complementary skills encouraged

## Registration

- **Registration Period:** 09 January — 22 February 2026
- **Prototype Submission Deadline:** 09 January — 01 March 2026
- **Registration Fee:** Completely **free**
- **Registration URL:** https://vision.hack2skill.com/event/amdslingshot/registration
